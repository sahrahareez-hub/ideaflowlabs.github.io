#  Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: April 2026**

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications developed and published by **Idea Flow Labs** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent user experience.

### 1. Our "Privacy First" Philosophy
Our apps are designed to function without requiring you to provide personal identifying information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. 

### 2. Information Collection and Use
While we do not collect personal data, our apps use third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device:
* **Google AdMob:** To provide advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers to personalize ads and measure performance.
* **Firebase Analytics:** To monitor app performance, technical crashes, and user engagement metrics.

### 3. Android Permissions
To provide full functionality, our apps may request the following permissions:
* **Internet & Network State:** Required to load currency rates and display advertisements.
* **Post Notifications:** Required for the Expiry Tracker to send you reminders.
* **Schedule Exact Alarm:** Required to ensure reminders trigger precisely at the time you set.
* **Receive Boot Completed:** Allows the app to reschedule your active reminders if your phone restarts.

### 4. Data Storage
Data entered into tools such as the **Expiry Tracker** or **Calculation History** is stored locally on your device's private storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted if the app is uninstalled.

### 5. Children’s Privacy
Our apps do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
