# Global Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: April 2026**

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications developed and published by **Idea Flow Labs** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent user experience across our suite of utility and conversion tools.

### 1. Our "Privacy First" Philosophy
Our apps are designed to function without requiring you to provide personal identifying information (PII). We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.

### 2. Information Collection and Use
While we do not collect personal data, our apps use third-party services for essential functionality:
* **Google AdMob:** Displays advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers to personalize ads.
* **Firebase Analytics:** Monitors app performance and technical crashes.
* **Currency Exchange API:** Some apps fetch real-time exchange rates. This requires a standard network request but does not transmit user data.

### 3. Android Permissions
To provide full functionality, our apps may request the following:
* **Internet & Network State:** Required to load live currency conversion rates and advertisements.
* **Post Notifications:** Required for the Expiry Tracker to send you reminders.
* **Schedule Exact Alarm:** Ensures reminders trigger precisely at the time you set.
* **Receive Boot Completed:** Re-schedules your active reminders if your device restarts.

### 4. Data Storage & Conversions
* **Conversion Data:** All mathematical conversions (Length, Weight, BMI, etc.) are processed locally on your device.
* **User Data:** Data entered into tools like the **Expiry Tracker** or **Calculation History** is stored in your device's private storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted if the app is uninstalled.

### 5. Children’s Privacy
Our apps are not designed for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
